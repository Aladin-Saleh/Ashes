◆ unnormalizedCoordinates

bool renderer::SamplerCreateInfo::unnormalizedCoordinates

Controls whether to use unnormalized or normalized texel coordinates to address texels of the image. When set to true, the range of the image coordinates used to lookup the texel is in the range of zero to the image dimensions for x, y and z. When set to false the range of image coordinates is zero to one.
